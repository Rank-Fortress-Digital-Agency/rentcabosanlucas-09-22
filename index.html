Romantic Activities for Couples in Cabo San Lucas

Read Post

Cabo San Lucas is an ideal romantic getaway destination. Picture relaxing by a luxurious private pool with your lover or evening walks along the beach under a beautiful sunset. This resort city is the epitome of a romantic vacation. However, other than stunning landscapes and sceneries, if you plan on visiting Cabo San Lucas, it’s essential to have an itinerary of activities.

In this post, we have carefully handpicked the most luxurious and private activities you can indulge in with your partner. It’s worth mentioning that Cabo San Lucas is packed with many activities. However, since this is a romantic escape, the ideal options should be intimate and cozy.

Private ATV Tours


RCSL can book you and your partner on an exclusive and romantic ATV tour. This thrilling and memorable activity allows the two of you to explore Cabo’s canyon trails, white sandy beaches, and oceanside cliffs. On your ATV tour, you can choose a suitable destination along the trail for a short picnic date.

If you and your better half have a competitive spirit, private ATV tours are an ideal activity. Competing with one another on various terrains is a healthy challenge that can help strengthen your relationship.

Spa Date

Another remarkable way of having fun in Cabo San Lucas as a couple is by going on a spa date. The best part about Cabo Spa services is that they can be offered in-villa or in one of their luxurious facilities. Most spas in Cabo have a ‘Just for Couples’ package. This option provides you with an opportunity to connect mentally and spiritually. If you have always wanted to surprise your partner with a royal treat, book them for a Swiss shower or a deep tissue massage.

Romantic Dinner Date


There are plenty of romantic dinner spots in Cabo. Most restaurants here have a fantastic ambiance, and the menus are pretty diverse. If you have had your fair share of junk and processed foods, you should try Cabo’s farm-to-table dining.

These are restaurants that serve fresh food straight from the farm. This isn’t just a regular dinner date. Most farm-to-table restaurants in Cabo San Lucas first take you on a trip to the farm. Then you can order, and your meals will be prepared in an open kitchen right before your eyes. A farm-to-table is an excellent bonding experience.

A Yacht Charter


Nothing beats renting a private yacht if you truly crave an intimate and unforgettable activity in Cabo. With your private boat, the two of you can set sail to the Sea of Cortez and adore some of its wonders, such as the Arch. You can enjoy a romantic dinner in the middle of the Ocean. If you plan to propose during your vacation in Cabo and want this moment to be special, we highly recommend a yacht charter.
